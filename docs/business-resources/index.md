## Key Principles for Safe AI Adoption

These resources are built around core principles:

1. **Start small**: Pilot projects before enterprise-wide deployment
2. **Build capability**: Invest in training alongside technology
3. **Manage change**: Address the human and organisational impacts
4. **Use guardrails**: Implement governance from day one
5. **Learn iteratively**: Treat AI adoption as a learning process
6. **Leverage support**: Take advantage of available funding and programs
